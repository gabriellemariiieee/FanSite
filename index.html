Dunkin' Donuts

Dunkin’ Donuts was founded in Quicy, MA by William Rosenberg in 1948. It’s a shop that sells donuts and coffee, as well as other food and drink, and has been a big part of my life for 17 years.

When I was four, my dad would take me there to get me a chocolate frosted with sprinkles for breakfast before daycare. I don’t remember much from being four years old but that is a vivid memory. Even to this day, the chocolate frosted is my favorite donut.

They have 14 flavors of donuts but introduce or return seasonal flavors, such as the heart-shaped brownie batter one during Valentine’s Day.

They have a wide variety of drinks, from hot and iced coffees, to teas, hot chocolates, and even refreshers and coolatas.

While mostly known for coffee and donuts, they also have a variety of breakfast foods. Bagels, muffins, various breakfast sandwhiches, hashbrowns, and avocado toast, just to name a few. And aside from the donuts, everything is made just how you want it.
